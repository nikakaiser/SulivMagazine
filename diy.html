DIY Closet Refresh

Instructions


STEP ONE: place cardboard between the front and back of the pant leg so the paint doesn’t bleed through
STEP TWO: lightly sketch out your designs in colored pencil
STEP THREE: trace your designs with the paint pen
TIPS:
• pull the fabric taut for a smoother drawing surface
• turn the pants inside out and use care when washing
• practice drawing out your designs on paper beforehand
